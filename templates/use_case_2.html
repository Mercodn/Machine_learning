Use Case 2: Diabetes Risk Prediction

Title

Predicting Diabetes Risk Using Patient Data

Problem Description

Diabetes is a growing global health concern. Early prediction of diabetes risk can enable preventive measures and lifestyle interventions to delay or prevent the onset of the disease.

Type of Machine Learning

Supervised Learning - Classification

Input Data
  • Age
  • BMI (Body Mass Index)
  • Blood pressure
  • Glucose levels
  • Family history
  • Physical activity levels
  • Dietary habits
Expected Output

Classification: High Risk or Low Risk of developing diabetes (binary classification)

Real-world Relevance

Early identification of at-risk individuals allows for targeted prevention programs, reducing healthcare costs and improving quality of life by preventing complications associated with diabetes.
