How to Interpret Results

Probability Interpretation

  • ≥ 80%: Very likely to make a purchase - Target with special offers
  • 60-80%: Likely to make a purchase - Send promotional emails
  • 40-60%: Uncertain - Provide additional information about products
  • 20-40%: Unlikely to purchase - Consider different marketing approach
  • < 20%: Very unlikely - Focus on other customer segments

Business Applications

  • Marketing Strategy: Allocate marketing budget based on purchase probability
  • Customer Segmentation: Group customers by likelihood to purchase
  • Personalization: Customize offers for high-probability customers
  • Campaign Optimization: Target resources toward likely converters

Feature Insights

  • Website Visits: More visits usually indicate higher purchase intent
  • Time on Site: Longer engagement suggests genuine interest
  • Previous Purchases: Repeat customers are likely to purchase again
  • Discount Usage: Shows price sensitivity and promotional responsiveness
