Use Case 4: AI-Assisted Drug Discovery

Title

Accelerating Drug Discovery with Machine Learning

Problem Description

Traditional drug discovery is time-consuming and expensive, taking 10-15 years and billions of dollars. ML can analyze molecular structures and predict drug-target interactions to identify promising candidates faster.

Type of Machine Learning

Supervised Learning - Classification/Regression (with Deep Learning)

Input Data
  • Molecular structures and properties
  • Protein target sequences
  • Historical drug trial data
  • Chemical compound databases
  • Bioactivity measurements
Expected Output

Prediction of drug efficacy, toxicity, and target binding affinity (multi-class classification/regression)

Real-world Relevance

ML-driven drug discovery can reduce development time and costs by 50-70%, enabling faster access to new treatments for diseases like cancer, Alzheimer's, and rare genetic disorders.
