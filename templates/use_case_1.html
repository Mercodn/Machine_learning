Use Case 1: AI-Powered Cancer Screening

Title

AI-Powered Cancer Screening and Detection

Problem Description

Cancer detection often requires expert radiologists to analyze medical images. AI can assist in identifying potential tumors and abnormalities more quickly and accurately, especially in screening programs where workload is high.

Type of Machine Learning

Supervised Learning - Classification (with Computer Vision)

Input Data
  • Medical images (X-rays, MRIs, CT scans)
  • Patient demographics (age, gender)
  • Historical medical data
Expected Output

Classification: Benign or Malignant (binary classification), with confidence scores

Real-world Relevance

This technology can significantly reduce false negatives in cancer screening, enable earlier detection, and help overburdened healthcare systems process more cases efficiently, ultimately saving lives through timely intervention.
