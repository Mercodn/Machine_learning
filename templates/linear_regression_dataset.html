Dataset Explanation

The dataset contains 1,338 records of insurance charges with the following features:

  • Age: Age of the primary beneficiary (18-64 years)
  • Sex: Gender of the beneficiary (male/female)
  • BMI: Body mass index (15.96-53.13)
  • Children: Number of children covered by insurance (0-5)
  • Smoker: Smoking status (yes/no)
  • Region: Residential area (northeast, northwest, southeast, southwest)
  • Charges: Individual medical costs billed by insurance (target variable)
