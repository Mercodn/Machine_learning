Evaluation Metrics

The model performance is evaluated using standard classification metrics calculated on the test set.

  • Accuracy: Overall correctness of predictions
  • Precision: Proportion of positive predictions that are actually positive
  • Recall: Proportion of actual positives that are correctly identified
  • F1-Score: Harmonic mean of precision and recall
  • AUC: Area under the ROC curve, measuring model's ability to distinguish classes
Interpretation of Results

The confusion matrix shows the number of correctly and incorrectly classified iris flowers. The ROC curve demonstrates the Perceptron's ability to distinguish between Setosa and Versicolor species.
