Machine Learning Use Cases

1. Fraud Detection in Banking
By identifying unusual patterns in transaction data, advanced machine learning models — including gradient boosting machines (e.g. XGBoost, LightGBM), deep neural networks, and unsupervised anomaly detection algorithms — can detect fraudulent activity in real time with high precision, significantly reducing false positives and protecting both financial institutions and genuine customers without interrupting normal usage.

2. Disease Detection with Computer Vision
Machine learning, particularly deep learning models trained via supervised learning, enables computers to recognize signs of diseases in medical images. A prominent real-world application is the automated detection of cancer (e.g., breast cancer in mammograms, lung cancer in CT scans, or skin cancer in dermoscopy images) with performance that in many cases rivals or exceeds that of human specialists.
